# Data

The experiments use public ultrasound datasets, but the datasets are not redistributed here.

Expected datasets:

- TN5000: thyroid nodule ultrasound classification data.
- BUSI: breast ultrasound images with lesion annotations.
- AUL: annotated ultrasound liver lesion data.

The included dataset-construction scripts expect local dataset roots and generate VOC-style ROI datasets or detector datasets. Edit the root paths in the scripts or pass equivalent paths before running.

No patient images, masks, or raw annotations are included in this repository beyond manuscript figures.
