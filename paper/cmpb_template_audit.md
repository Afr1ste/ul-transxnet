# CMPB Template and Submission Audit

Checked on 2026-05-10.

## Submission timing

Computer Methods and Programs in Biomedicine uses regular online submission through Editorial Manager. The official Guide for Authors and Editorial Manager page show an active manuscript-submission route. I found no fixed deadline for ordinary Original Research submissions, so a manuscript prepared in the next few days can be submitted as a regular article.

The ScienceDirect article-collections page lists a special issue/call for papers, "Exploring the Frontiers of Radiomics: Unveiling Novel Insights through Advanced Techniques and Multimodal Approaches", with a submission deadline of 30 March 2027. This is a special-issue deadline, not a deadline for ordinary CMPB submissions.

## Format requirements applied

- Journal: `Computer Methods and Programs in Biomedicine`.
- Source format: editable `.tex` source using Elsevier `elsarticle`; PDF alone is not a source file.
- Layout: review-mode single-column LaTeX draft.
- Article type: Original Research.
- Title page includes article title, author names, affiliations, and one corresponding author.
- Abstract uses CMPB structured headings: Background and Objective, Methods, Results, Conclusions.
- Keywords: 3--6 English keywords.
- Highlights prepared as a separate editable file.
- Separate title-page file prepared in `title_page_cmpb.md`; the corresponding-author telephone number remains author-side information to fill before upload.
- Numbered sections are used.
- References use Elsevier numbered style through `elsarticle-num`.
- Cover letter prepared separately with required points: why CMPB, what is known, what the study adds, and confirmation that the paper is not published or under consideration elsewhere.
- CRediT, ethics, funding, competing interest, generative-AI declaration, and data-availability sections are included.

## Remaining author-side fields

- Confirm final author order and affiliations.
- Fill corresponding author's phone number in `cover_letter_cmpb.md`.
- Fill corresponding author's phone number in `title_page_cmpb.md`.
- Decide whether the submission will include supplementary material or only main-text figures/tables.
- Confirm repository URL and data-sharing wording before submission.
- Re-run final PDF proof after any author edits.
