\section{Reproducibility Note}
\label{sec:appendix_sources}

All main benchmark tables were generated from the seed/fold summary CSV files used by the experiment pipeline. The project archive records the dataset split manifests, ROI construction scripts, training entry points, aggregation scripts, model checkpoints, Android deployment artifacts, and a table-level source index required to reproduce the reported TN5000, BUSI, AUL, and Android deployment results.
